🧮 GST Calculator India

India's GST system can be confusing with multiple tax rates (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) and component taxes (CGST, SGST, IGST). Our GST calculator instantly shows you the exact breakdown of taxes on any amount. Whether you're a small business owner filing returns, a freelancer invoicing clients, or a consumer checking restaurant bills, our calculator provides accurate results every time.

The calculator supports inclusive and exclusive GST calculations, reverse GST calculations, and provides detailed breakdowns of CGST, SGST, and IGST components. It's particularly useful for e-commerce sellers, retailers, and service providers who need to quickly calculate tax obligations.

🎮 Gaming Currency Calculators

Gaming has become a massive industry with billions spent on in-game currencies annually. Our gaming calculators help players understand the real-world value of virtual currencies like Robux (Roblox), V-Bucks (Fortnite), Minecoins (Minecraft), FIFA Points, COD Points, and Apex Coins.

Parents use these calculators to set reasonable spending limits for their children. Gamers use them to find the best value packages and avoid overspending. Content creators use them to explain costs to their audiences. Each calculator includes current pricing tiers, best value recommendations, and historical rate changes.

We update our rates regularly to reflect official pricing changes and regional variations. Whether you're converting 1,000 Robux to USD or comparing V-Bucks packages, our calculators provide instant, accurate conversions with support for multiple currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD).

🎖️ VA Disability Calculator

Veterans Affairs disability compensation can be complex, especially when calculating combined ratings and dependent benefits. Our VA Disability Calculator uses the official 2025 VA compensation rates and the exact combined ratings formula used by the VA to provide accurate monthly payment estimates.

Veterans use our calculator to estimate their benefits before filing claims, understand how additional disabilities might affect their ratings, and plan their finances. The calculator accounts for all variables including marital status, number of children, children in school, dependent parents, and special circumstances like Aid & Attendance.

We include comprehensive guides explaining VA math, the combined ratings formula, and how dependent benefits work. This helps veterans understand not just what they'll receive, but why the calculations work the way they do. The calculator is updated annually with new COLA adjustments to ensure accuracy.
