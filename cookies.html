How We Use Cookies

Forge APIs uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you signed in to your account securely
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: To understand how our services are used and improve them
  • Performance: To monitor and optimize the performance of our website

Important: We use cookies minimally and only when necessary. Our API services themselves do not use cookies - they use API key authentication in headers.

Cookie Consent

For EU/UK Visitors

  • We will ask for your consent before setting any non-essential cookies
  • Essential cookies do not require consent as they're necessary for the website to function
  • You can withdraw your consent at any time through cookie settings
  • We respect "Do Not Track" browser settings

For Other Regions

  • By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies
  • You can still manage cookies through your browser settings

Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of these cookies as they are strictly necessary for delivering services to you.

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use this information to improve our services and user experience. You can opt-out of these cookies.

Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and settings. If you do not allow these cookies, some or all of these services may not function properly.

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies individually or entirely
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies entirely (not recommended)
  • Set your browser to ask before accepting cookies

How to Manage Cookies in Popular Browsers

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Opera

Important: Blocking all cookies may prevent you from accessing certain features of our website, particularly the dashboard and account management areas. Essential cookies are required for these features to work.

Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of specific cookie types:

Analytics Cookies

  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
  • Use your browser's "Do Not Track" setting (we honor this)
  • Use our cookie settings panel (click button above)
  • Contact us at privacy@forgeapis.com to request exclusion

Marketing Cookies

We do not currently use marketing or advertising cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and request your consent.

API Services and Cookies

Important for API Users

Our API services are completely cookie-free:

  • No cookies are set when making API requests
  • No cookies are required for API authentication
  • API responses do not include cookie headers
  • Your API integrations are not affected by cookie settings
  • GDPR cookie consent is not required for API usage
  • Authentication is handled via API keys in request headers

This makes our APIs ideal for privacy-conscious applications and simplifies compliance with privacy regulations.
