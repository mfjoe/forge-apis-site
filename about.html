About ForgeAPIs

Free calculators that actually work

Our Mission

ForgeAPIs provides free, accurate calculators without the typical annoyances of calculator sites. No forced signups, no paywalls, no outdated data - just tools that work.

What We Offer

  • Gaming Calculators: Currency converters for V-Bucks, Robux, COD Points, FIFA Points, Apex Coins, and Minecoins, plus FPS, DPI, and monitor calculators
  • Financial Tools: GST calculator for Indian businesses, tip calculators with bill splitting
  • Benefits Calculators: VA disability calculator with 2025 official rates

Why We're Different

We update our rates monthly from official sources. Every calculator is completely free with no premium versions. Your privacy matters - calculations run in your browser, we don't collect or store your data.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, ForgeAPIs started when we got frustrated with existing calculator sites covered in ads and requiring accounts for basic functions. We built something better - clean, fast, accurate calculators that respect users' time and intelligence.
